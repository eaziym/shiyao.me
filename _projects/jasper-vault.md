## Overview

As a Business Analyst Intern at Jasper Vault Technology, a Singapore-based crypto fund, I developed analytics dashboards, optimized data workflows, and researched algorithmic trading strategies to support investment decision-making.

## The Journey

### 1. Problem Identification

**Challenges in Crypto Fund Management:**
- Complex on-chain data scattered across multiple sources
- Need for real-time market intelligence
- Manual data retrieval and analysis was time-consuming
- Required actionable insights for weekly investment meetings
- Needed to identify smart money movements and trends

**Goals:**
- Centralize crypto market analytics
- Automate data collection and analysis
- Provide actionable insights for fund management
- Enable data-driven investment decisions

**Developed comprehensive dashboards for:**

#### Ethereum (ETH) Staking Analytics

- Yield analysis and projections

#### Smart Money Holdings Analysis
- Whale wallet tracking

- Position changes and accumulation patterns
- Risk and opportunity identification

#### Crypto Market Trends
- Market sentiment indicators
- Trading volume analysis
- Price correlation studies
- Sector-specific performance metrics

**Technologies:** Dune SQL, Data Visualization, Blockchain Analytics

**Application:**

- Identified market opportunities and risks
- Tracked portfolio-relevant metrics

### 3. Data Workflow Optimization

**Problem:**
- Multiple API providers with different formats

**Solution:**
Streamlined data workflows by:

**Technologies:** Python, Power Query, API Integration

**Benefits:**

- Reusable workflows
- Significant time savings
